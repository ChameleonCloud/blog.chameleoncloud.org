<p dir="ltr">As part of Phase 3 we’re looking to improve the Chameleon experience and make the system generally better over the next 4 years. This is your chance to guide hardware purchases, influence Chameleon capabilities and change priorities in our roadmap. </p>

<p dir="ltr">To help us in this effort, we would like to invite you to take this 10-15 minute survey, and share your experiences, desires, pain points and anything else to help us build a better Chameleon for you! The survey will be open till the end of June. </p>

<p dir="ltr">Thank you in advance for your participation!</p>

<p dir="ltr"> </p>

<p dir="ltr">The survey is available here: <a href="https://bit.ly/34E5N64">2021 Chameleon User Survey</a></p>
