<p style="text-align: center;"><img media="" src="https://chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public/6c/a6/6ca6e73f-68ac-42b1-be86-de51bc6d59a4/hackathon-600x400.jpg" style=""></p>

<p>We are delighted to offer a limited number of registration reimbursement grants for the upcoming <a href="https://repeto.cs.uchicago.edu/2023/08/08/announcing-reproducibility-hackathon-at-the-ic2e-conference-september-25th-in-boston/">Practical Reproducibility Tutorial/Hackathon at the IC2E Conference</a>, which takes place in Boston, MA on September 25, 2023. Grant recipients will be expected to register for the tutorial and apply for reimbursement after tutorial attendance. Only tutorial  registration will be supported at this time; the grant is therefore most relevant to local participants. <br>
 <br>
To indicate your interest in a registration reimbursement grant, please complete the <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeJHMzvztwjRGJw6Wv7NhuLWxZi1EWJBXXIHU-3fbhkvrg45A/viewform?usp=sf_link">application form</a> by September 5th, 2023.<br>
 <br>
Awarded applicants will be notified no later than September 8th, 2023. Preference will be given to individuals in order of application submission and the quality of justification/interest in packaging experiments for reproducibility.<br>
 </p>
