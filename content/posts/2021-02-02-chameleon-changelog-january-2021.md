<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Dear Chameleon users,</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Hey! It's your friendly neighborhood Chameleon changelog, back from a long vacation. We'll be sending monthly changelogs out all year to keep you informed on the latest and greatest developments on the testbed. Here's what we've put together for you this month:</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">General availability for CHI@NU!</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> The CHI@NU site has been available for Early Users on a “by request” basis for the last six months or so while we have been working out methods of adding an associate site to Chameleon easily. We are happy to announce that the Chameleon Associate Site at Northwestern is now fully integrated into the rest of the testbed and can be used by any Chameleon project or user. Last year we </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2019/06/03/chameleon-changelog-may-2019/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">highlighted</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> the Mellanox ConnectX-5 100GE network cards available on nodes there. The CHI@NU site offers more opportunity for advanced and high-performance networking experiments, and can additionally be </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/technical/networks/networks_stitching.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">stitched</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> to the existing CHI@TACC and CHI@UC sites for dedicated wide-area experiments. Interested in becoming an associate site yourself? </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/user/help/ticket/new/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Contact us</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> or learn more about CHI-in-a-Box, our packaged deployment of Chameleon that you can deploy yourself, </span><a href="https://github.com/chameleoncloud/chi-in-a-box" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">here</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">New Cascade Lake hardware is now available at TACC.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> Late last year, we </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2019/12/03/chameleon-changelog-november-2019/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">announced</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> the preliminary installation of one rack of Intel Cascade Lake CPUs. We are happy to announce that three more racks, for a grand total of 56 nodes, are now available at CHI@TACC for your experimentation. These new "Cascade Lake R" compute nodes include the Xeon Gold 6240R Cascade Lake Scalable Performance Refresh CPUs with support for 96 threads! As usual, you can find all of the hardware details in the </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/hardware/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Chameleon hardware catalogue</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Orchestrate your experiments with Chameleon's official Python library. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Particularly enterprising users may have discovered that, while there has been a Python library called "python-chi" for some time, its API was still under development and we never had proper documentation for it. We're happy to announce that python-chi is now ready for prime time, complete with </span><a href="https://python-chi.readthedocs.io/en/latest/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">full documentation</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and some </span><a href="https://python-chi.readthedocs.io/en/latest/notebooks/reservations.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">helpful usage examples</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> to get you started. With the library, you can make reservations, launch new bare metal instances, and manage network abstractions for networking experiments. Let us know how you like it, and please </span><a href="https://github.com/ChameleonCloud/python-chi/issues" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">file issues</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> for any bugs you happen to encounter.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">CUDA gets turned up to 11. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">We've released a new suite of base images for </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/appliances/92/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">CentOS</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/appliances/90/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Ubuntu</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> with the latest CUDA 11 release for all of your GPU experimentation. These images should be launched on our "gpu_*" class nodes, like the V100s we </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/blog/2020/12/31/2020-end-year-changelog/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">recently added</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> to CHI@UC.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">See all publications using Chameleon! </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">We're entering phase three of the testbed and have collected </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/about/chameleon-used-research/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">a list of the publications that have cited the testbed over the years</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, to give you a sense of how everybody is using Chameleon to do interesting research. As a reminder, please cite Chameleon when you are publishing! </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/learn/frequently-asked-questions/#toc-how-should-i-acknowledge-or-reference-chameleon-" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Refer to our official citation guidance</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></p>
