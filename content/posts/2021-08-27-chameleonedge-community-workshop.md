<p><strong>Date:</strong> September 9, 2021</p>

<p><b>Registration: </b>The registration is free and the workshop is open to all, but please bear in mind that <strong>registration</strong><strong> will close on August 31st</strong>. Register at: <b> </b><a href="https://forms.gle/VmbaCquWMNXe7bCs6">https://forms.gle/VmbaCquWMNXe7bCs6</a></p>

<h3>Event Details</h3>

<p>On September 9th, Chameleon will host a virtual community workshop to discuss emergent research directions and application requirements driving the requirements for an edge testbed. </p>

<p>The goal of this workshop is to determine the shapre and priorities for an edge testbed with the involvement of broad scientific community. We will have a presentation on the current <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/experiment/chiedge/">CHI@Edge</a> offering, keynotes setting out research directions and plans for their support, and most importantly, presentations from <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/experiment/chiedge/">CHI@Edge</a> users describing their work. This workshop is targeted to all interested in edge computing in Computer Science research, education, and innovative applications. </p>
