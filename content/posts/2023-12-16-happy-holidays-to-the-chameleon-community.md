<p><img height="auto" src="https://chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public/44/f2/44f2f9e7-6190-4428-9a6e-76c232a1de87/chameleon_christmas_2023.png" width="50%"></p>

<p>As we approach the end of the year, we want to extend our heartfelt gratitude for your continued support and valuable contributions. Your innovative projects and insightful feedback have been instrumental in enhancing the Chameleon testbed. For those who haven't yet explored, we encourage you to visit our <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/blog/category/user-experiments/">user experiment blogs</a> to discover the fascinating work our community has accomplished this year.</p>

<p>We're honored to be part of such a dynamic and industrious community and eagerly anticipate collaborating with you in 2024.</p>

<p>Please note a few important updates as we transition into the holiday season:</p>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Holiday Schedule for Chameleon Help Desk</strong>: Our Help Desk will be taking a brief hiatus starting December 24, resuming operations on January 3. While our team enjoys a well-deserved break, rest assured that we'll return refreshed and ready to assist you with any challenges that may arise in the new year.</li>
	<li><strong>"Chameleon for Christmas" Tradition Continues</strong>: We're delighted to announce the return of our popular 'double lease' offer! From noon on December 19th to noon on December 22nd, you can request 14-day leases, including access to our GPU nodes. This offer allows your experiments to progress diligently over the holidays, setting you up for exciting discoveries upon return. Remember, this opportunity expires at noon on December 22nd, so plan accordingly!</li>
</ul>

<p>We wish you a joyful holiday season and look forward to another year of groundbreaking work.</p>

<p>Warm regards,<br>
The Chameleon Team</p>
