<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Dear Chameleon users,</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">I think it's fair to say most everybody had a truly awful year at some level. Without minimizing that, I wanted to take a brief moment to reflect with gratitude on what went right this year, at least in the (small) realm of our work on this research testbed. So here’s one year in review, representing combined “changelog” information from the entire year.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Four more years of Chameleon! </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">First things first: Chameleon wrapped up the end of Phase 2 this year, and we are so thankful that we are able to continue developing and operating this infrastructure for you all. Phase 3 began just a few months ago and we're already hard at work designing new capabilities and improvements, which have been shaped by your valuable feedback, requirements and use cases over Phase 2.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Support for identity federation. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">As many of you are aware, we transitioned all Chameleon legacy accounts to federated identity over the last months (thanks again for bearing with us on that--that was a bear of a project!) One reason for doing this is that it makes it easier for you to use a variety of existing and emergent research infrastructure, such as </span><a href="https://www.cloudbank.org" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">CloudBank</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> or </span><a href="https://whatisfabric.net" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">FABRIC</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> with a single identity--allowing you to move across these infrastructures with greater ease when constructing experiments that leverage resources distributed across them. We're looking forward to building stronger ties between Chameleon and these exciting new resources!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">New hardware. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Most of the phase 2 hardware at the CHI@UC site got GPUs installed, </span><a href="https://lists.chameleoncloud.org/mailman/private/users/2020-February/000425.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">with 40 RTX 6000 cards installed into existing nodes</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and </span><a href="https://lists.chameleoncloud.org/mailman/private/users/2020-October/000446.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">3 new V100 nodes coming online in November</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">. At TACC, we started adding </span><a href="https://lists.chameleoncloud.org/mailman/private/users/2020-November/000456.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">new Cascade Lake nodes</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> equipped with </span><a href="https://ark.intel.com/content/www/us/en/ark/products/199343/intel-xeon-gold-6240r-processor-35-75m-cache-2-40-ghz.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Intel Xeon Gold 6240R processors</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, 192GB of RAM, and a 480GB SSD; ultimately 56 of those will be added at TACC and they will replace our aging Haswell nodes. As always, you can find out more about their specs in </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/hardware/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">our resource catalogue</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Support for AWS DirectConnect in experiments. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">This was a fun one… </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2020/07/10/chameleon-experiments-using-direct-network-connections-public-clouds-aws/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">in February we finished</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> work that allows Chameleon experiments to peer directly with AWS via Internet2's CloudConnect. Chameleon eliminates the need to involve campus IT staff and enables access to direct public cloud network connections to all Chameleon users. Thus, the identity federation with CloudBank is not just about theoretical potential -- any Chameleon user can now easily use resources from both of these NSF-sponsored infrastructures without the need for complicated campus networking configuration! </span><a href="https://jupyter.chameleoncloud.org/hub/user-redirect/lab/tree/notebooks/tutorials/networking/CloudConnect.ipynb" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Try it out in the Jupyter Notebook here.</span></a></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Expanded SDN experiment capabilities.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> In January we also made significant improvements to the </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/technical/networks/networks_sdn.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">SDN capabilities</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> on Chameleon, making it possible to deploy SDN experiments using any of our nodes (prior to that, it was only possible to run SDN experiments using our Skylake nodes). Furthermore, these improvements also unlocked interesting new experiments leveraging the fact that many of our Haswell nodes have </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/technical/networks/networks_basic.html#multiple-networks" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">two configurable network interfaces</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Trovi: an experiment sharing platform. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">In November </span><a href="https://lists.chameleoncloud.org/mailman/private/users/2020-November/000455.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">we officially announced Trovi</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, the Chameleon reproducibility platform that allows Chameleon users to discover and share packaged experiments. You can even publish your packaged experiment to </span><a href="https://zenodo.org/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Zenodo</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> for long-term storage and citation. Trovi is integrated with Chameleon’s Jupyter installation, so if you're familiar with Jupyter Notebooks, you'll be right at home. If you're new to notebooks, check out </span><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mzxkan8AZIQ" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">this webinar from last year</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, which illustrates how Notebooks can be used to organize and orchestrate CISE experiments. We have had several users already taking advantage of this new capability to organize their experimental workflow and leverage the artifacts to improve the quality of their paper submissions. In particular, last summer we worked with a group of talented students who reproduced and packaged experiments from foundational papers (such as</span><a href="https://dawn.cs.stanford.edu/pubs/coleman2017dawnbench.pdf" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style=""> DAWNBench</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">) that can now be used for further research or education. They have written about their experiences </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2020/11/01/chameleon-and-reproducibility-linnos-case-study/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">here</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2020/11/23/reproducing-solid-state-drive-simulator-research-results-chameleon/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">here</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">--check it out!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">General system upgrades. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Much of this year was focused on “behind the scenes” work  geared towards making the system better. We upgraded all our installations to the latest OpenStack release (Train) to leverage bug fixes and performance improvements around instance launch times.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">This is true of our CHI installations on both sites as well as our KVM platform. We also made various usability improvements in the reservation system as well as user interface, improved the system defaults, and generally worked on making the system more reliable and responsive.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Appliances.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> We added multiple appliances this year (</span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/appliances/83/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">CC-CentOS8</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/appliances/84/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">CC-CentOS8-CUDA10</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> being the most important). We also installed Python 3 in all Chameleon-supported images and set as default (due to Python 2 end-of-life in early 2020), made improvements to the cc-snapshot tool, updated the </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/appliances/77/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">DevStack appliance</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> for the OpenStack Rocky release, and improved </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/technical/networks/networks_basic.html#firewall" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">default security rules</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> for all our base images, to reduce chance of accidental compromise.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Cloud trace data.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> We have updated both our open-source trace generator and the trace format specification to serve for both the virtual machine and bare-metal clouds. We also published a </span><a href="https://zenodo.org/record/4015338" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">new KVM trace</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> from our KVM site and </span><a href="https://zenodo.org/record/4015358" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">bare-metal traces</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> from the CHI@UC site and CHI@TACC site using the updated trace format version.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-a8c38acb-7fff-582c-283e-ec6b36385905" style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">More IPs for more experiments. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">We obtained and added another 100+ IP addresses to the CHI@UC site in response to increased demand. If you are still having issues reserving multiple IP addresses for your experiment, check out </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2019/02/27/save-planet-use-fewer-ips/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">this blog post</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> for some ideas on how to make use of fewer. Reduce, re-use, recycle!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Reaching out. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">We sought to broaden our communication with users via webinars (most of which are recorded on our </span><a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/ChameleonCloud" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">YouTube channel</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">), social media, and </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">our blog</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">. We also increasingly invest in tools that allow our users to use Chameleon as an education platform; this year the </span><a href="https://csinparallel.org/csinparallel/workshops/Virtual20/index.html" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">CSInParallel</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and the </span><a href="https://www.anl.gov/education/big-data-camp" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">ANL Big Data Camp</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> took advantage of those capabilities. </span></p>

We will be releasing monthly changelogs again in the new year so you can keep abreast of the latest updates.</span></p>
