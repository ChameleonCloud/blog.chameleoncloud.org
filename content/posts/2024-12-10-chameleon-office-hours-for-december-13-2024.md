<p dir="ltr">To provide support to our users, we are piloting by appointment Chameleon Office Hours. The first session will be on December 13, 2024, from 10:00-11:00 am and 2:30-3:30 pm Central time. Office hours appointments can be scheduled for 20 minute intervals, at least 8 hours in advance. To schedule an appointment, see <a href="https://calendly.com/markpowers-uchicago/chameleon">this link</a>.</p>

<p dir="ltr">As always, if you need help outside of this time, please contact <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/user/help/">our help desk</a>. Additionally, you can find useful information about Chameleon on <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/blog/">our blog</a>, in our <a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/">documentation</a>, and on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ChameleonCloud/videos">YouTube</a>.</p>
