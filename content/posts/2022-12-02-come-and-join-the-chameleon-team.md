<p>You've enjoyed using the testbed, you've seen its power -- and its warts -- in all their glory: how about contributing back? Come and fix the warts, make strange and wonderful new hardware available to all, and work with a fun and dedicated team!</p>

<p>Our team is growing and we are looking for new team members to join us! We've got new projects in edge computing, reproducibility, and developing new digital materials for data systems -- and hope that they might tempt you to help us develop new scientific instruments and thereby create new opportunities for exploration. As individual reserachers we can only do so much -- but building a scientific instrument that enables the research of thousands of scientists is a great multiplier -- help us make the world a more interesting place and see great science done every day!</p>

<p>We currently have two positions open -- take a look, and if this is something that looks like a good match for your skills and interests, we'd love to talk to you!</p>
