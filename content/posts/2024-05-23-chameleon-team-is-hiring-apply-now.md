<h4>Join Our Team: Senior Research Software Engineer Position Open</h4>

<p><img src="https://chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public_thumbnails/filer_public/51/1d/511d0d87-5ae4-4457-8121-e713be049c5f/chameleon-at-work.png__400x300_q85_subsampling-2_upscale.jpg"></p>

<p><big><strong>UPDATE 08/16/2024 - Position is now closed. Thank you for applying!</strong></big></p>

<p><a href="https://nimbusproject.org/jobs/">Apply on the Nimbus Project website!</a></p>

<p>Are you ready to shape the future of scientific research? The Nimbus team, known for its pioneering work in cloud and edge computing, is looking for a Senior Research Software Engineer to join our dynamic group. This position offers the unique opportunity to work on the Chameleon platform, among other things, where you can contribute to transformative solutions that enhance scientific discovery.</p>

<p><strong>What We Offer:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Innovative Environment:</strong> Engage with an international research community exploring cutting-edge solutions in cloud computing, IoT, and more.</li>
	<li><strong>Impactful Work:</strong> Directly impact scientific research by developing and operating experimental platforms like Chameleon.</li>
	<li><strong>Collaborative Culture:</strong> Work alongside dedicated professionals in a friendly, challenge-oriented environment at a leading academic institution.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>We’re Looking For Someone Who:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li>Has at least four years of programming experience (development, debugging, and maintenance) in Unix/Linux environments.</li>
	<li>Is proficient in Python and has hands-on experience with IaaS cloud systems such as OpenStack or Amazon Web Services.</li>
	<li>Possesses strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to think independently and creatively to develop exceptional technical solutions.</li>
	<li>Is passionate about working on challenges that don't have obvious solutions and has a keen interest in advancing scientific research through innovative computing solutions.</li>
	<li>Holds a bachelor's degree in computer science or another relevant computer-related field, with an advanced degree in mathematics, computer science, electrical engineering, or a related field strongly preferred.</li>
</ul>

<p>Make a real difference in how scientific research is conducted and experienced. Learn more about this exciting opportunity and apply through our <a href="https://nimbusproject.org/jobs/">Nimbus team website</a>.</p>
