<p><img height="auto" src="https://chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public/d4/78/d4780460-cc1b-45e7-95a1-87a29106f70b/dalle_2024-02-06_165049.png" width="40%"></p>

<p>Chameleon is proud to support the <a href="http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/2024/index.html">BigDataX program</a>, a Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) site in Chicago Illinois. <strong>BigDataX is seeking students for its Summer 2024 cohort</strong>. The program has <a href="http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/2024/index.html#Mentors">four mentors</a> at <a href="http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/2024/index.html#Institutions">two institutions</a>, the Illinois Institute of Technology and University of Chicago, with a variety of complementing expertise from programming languages, parallel computing, distributed systems, data science, and edge computing; more information on potential projects can be found at <a href="http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/2024/index.html#Projects">http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/2024/index.html#Projects</a> (see also: <a href="http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/bigdatax.pdf">http://datasys.cs.iit.edu/grants/BigDataX/bigdatax.pdf</a>). The program will be conducted in-person and will unfortunately not be able to accomodate a virtual format. <strong>Applications are due March 4, 2024. See the details for applying below.</strong></p>

<p><strong>Apply here: </strong><a href="https://etap.nsf.gov/award/1882/opportunity/8813">https://etap.nsf.gov/award/1882/opportunity/8813</a></p>

<h2>Who are the REU mentors?</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Ioan Raicu (IIT)</li>
	<li>Kyle Hale (IIT)</li>
	<li>Kate Keahey (UChicago)</li>
	<li>Kyle Chard (UChicago) </li>
</ul>

<h2>Project Areas</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Data Management in Scientific Computing and Blockchains</li>
	<li>Data Analytics and Systems</li>
	<li>Operating Systems &amp; Computer Architecture</li>
	<li>Edge Computing with CHI@Edge</li>
</ul>

<h2>Eligibility Requirements</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Undergraduate student in Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, or related discipline at a US institution</li>
	<li>US citizen or permanent resident on or before May 28th, 2024</li>
	<li>Minimum GPA 3.0</li>
	<li>2 recommendation forms; must be received by March 4th, 2024</li>
</ul>

<h2>Important Dates</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Application deadline: March 4th, 2024</li>
	<li>Recommendations: March 4th, 2024</li>
	<li>Notification of decision: March 18th, 2024</li>
	<li>Program starts: May 28th, 2024</li>
	<li>Program ends: August 2nd, 2024</li>
</ul>

<h2>Program Highlights</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Spend 10 weeks immersed in research on Big Data at either Illinois Institute of Technology or University of Chicago</li>
	<li>Work on large scale distributed systems (e.g. Amazon AWS Cloud, Chameleon Cloud, Aurora Supercomputer, Mystic)</li>
	<li>Prepare for graduate school</li>
	<li>Publish papers and attend international conferences</li>
	<li>Finances (per summer):
	<ul>
		<li>$7000 stipend (taxable as income)</li>
		<li>$500 travel funds to attend conference (e.g. IEEE/ACM Supercomputing/SC 2024)</li>
		<li>$500 relocation allowance for travel to and from the BigDataX site (minimum of 50 miles away)</li>
		<li>Housing and meal plan included on the IIT campus paid directly to IIT housing (a $2250 value, paid by the university)</li>
	</ul>
	</li>
</ul>

<h2>Online Application</h2>

<p>To apply to the program, please submit your complete online application through ETAP by the March 4th 2024 deadline at <a href="https://etap.nsf.gov/award/1882/opportunity/8813">https://etap.nsf.gov/award/1882/opportunity/8813</a>. For any questions, please send email to <a href="mailto:bigdatax-group@iit.edu">bigdatax-group@iit.edu</a>.</p>
