<p>We are preparing for phase 2 of Chameleon and need the help of Chameleon users in determining what new hardware resources we should buy and what new capabilities we should provide.</p>

<p>To tell us about it, we invite Chameleon users to fill out a short survey. We made it as much like a conversation as we could: each question has a “Tell us more” open feedback option, and while many of them also have multiple choice answers, please treat them as conversation starters rather than an exhaustive list. You can skip any question that you feel is not relevant to you.</p>

<p>Look out for the survey URL which you should have received via our users mailing list, to which you are automatically subscribed as part of the Chameleon account creation process. If you have not received this email, let us know via our help desk.</p>

<p>Please provide your feedback by the end of February. Thank you in advance for your participation!</p>
