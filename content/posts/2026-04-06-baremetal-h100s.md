We are excited to announce that Chameleon now has two baremetal H100 nodes at CHI@TACC! Last year, we announced [H100 GPUs with virtual machines](https://blog.chameleoncloud.org/posts/chameleon-changelog-for-may-2025/) at KVM@TACC, but now we have this same hardware configured for baremetal on CHI@TACC, letting you run H100 experiments without going through a virtualization layer.

These nodes are Dell PowerEdge XE9640 equipped with Intel Xeon Platinum processor with 4 NVIDIA HGX H100 GPUs, 1 TB DDR5-4800 RAM, 2x 447.13 GB PCIe NVMe and 1x 3.84 TB PCIe NVMe. For the full hardware specification, [filter by `gpu_h100` in our hardware browser](https://chameleoncloud.org/hardware/).

To reserve these nodes, you can make a reservation directly from the [availability calendar](https://chi.tacc.chameleoncloud.org/project/leases/calendar/host/?node_type=gpu_h100) or by filtering `node_type=gpu_h100`.
