<p>We would like to invite you to come to the 2nd Chameleon User Meeting to be held on September 13-14 2017 in the Theory and Computer Science building at the Argonne National Laboratory!<br>
<br>
The purpose of the event is to create a forum where users can share their experiences working with the testbed, discuss challenges, and propose improvements. To facilitate this discussion we are soliciting proposals for brief presentations based on your work with Chameleon in the form of a 1-2 page abstract. For the top 10 selected abstracts we will reimburse the travel expenses of up to $1,000 for the presenting authors (one author per abstract).<br>
<br>
More information about the meeting can be found on the meeting web page at: <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/user-meeting-2017">https://www.chameleoncloud.org/user-meeting-2017</a><br>
<br>
We hope to see you in September!</p>
