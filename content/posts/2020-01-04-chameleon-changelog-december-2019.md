<p dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-8e9b6791-7fff-b468-2d09-f6fe5f1e5ee1" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Great news in Chameleon-land!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">From everyone at Chameleon, we hope you had a pleasant holiday and welcome to the new year! This update’s a short one due to all the festivities happening of late.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Floating DNS hostnames available for HTTPS experiments. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">If you’ve been exposing web interfaces over the public internet, you may have already played around with generating a self-signed certificate to secure your traffic. Now there is a better way: you can use a stable hostname assigned to your </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/technical/networks/networks_basic.html#floating-ip-addresses" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">Floating IP</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">. This allows you to e.g. use </span><a href="https://letsencrypt.org/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">LetsEncrypt’s</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> DNS verification to generate a certificate signed by a trusted CA. For more information about this, please refer to our </span><a href="https://chameleoncloud.readthedocs.io/en/latest/technical/networks/networks_basic.html#floating-dns-records" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">documentation</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"> </p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Legacy KVM environment being decommissioned this month. </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">As we </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2019/11/05/chameleon-changelog-october-2019/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">announced</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, the old KVM environment is being replaced by </span><a href="https://kvm.tacc.chameleoncloud.org/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">a new deployment</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, and you should work to migrate your workloads. We will be doing the final teardown of the old cluster this month.</span></p>
