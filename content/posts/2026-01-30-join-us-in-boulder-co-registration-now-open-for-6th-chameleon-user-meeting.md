<p>We're excited to announce that registration is now open for the <b>6th Chameleon User Meeting</b>, taking place <b>April 15-16, 2026</b> at the NCAR Mesa Lab in Boulder, Colorado!</p>

<p><b>Register soon to take advantage of early pricing (ends Feb. 28):</b> <a data-outlook-id="7dccfd51-2d79-461e-b007-8e8956724321" href="https://www.eventbrite.com/e/6th-chameleon-user-meeting-tickets-1982009337282" title="https://www.eventbrite.com/e/6th-chameleon-user-meeting-tickets-1982009337282">https://www.eventbrite.com/e/6th-chameleon-user-meeting-tickets-1982009337282</a></p>

<p><img src="https://chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public/6f/d0/6fd04df3-2a41-4293-b0a9-2c53ad5ed87c/mesa_lab.png" width="50%"></p>

<p>This year's meeting focuses on understanding the infrastructure needs for <b>computer science research and education in AI</b>. We're bringing together researchers, educators, and students from across the CS community to share experiences, discuss challenges, and help shape the future of AI research infrastructure. As with previous user meetings, we will produce a community report summarizing the infrastructure needs and vision for supporting CS research and education in AI.</p>

<p>The workshop will feature presentations from our community, keynote speakers, tutorials for users, plenty of opportunities to network, focused mini-symposia on AI education and reproducibility in AI, and beautiful Colorado mountain views.</p>

<p><b>Reminder - Call for Presentations - Submit by Feb. 16, 2026 11:59 PM AOE:</b> <b id="docs-internal-guid-b1505464-7fff-bb84-5395-dec02356e459"><a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/blog/2025/12/13/call-for-presentations-chameleon-user-meeting-2026/">https://chameleoncloud.org/blog/2025/12/13/call-for-presentations-chameleon-user-meeting-2026/</a></b></p>
