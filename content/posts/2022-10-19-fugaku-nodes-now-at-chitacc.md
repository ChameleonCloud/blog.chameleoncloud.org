<p dir="ltr">Interested in the <a href="https://www.fujitsu.com/global/about/innovation/fugaku/">Fugaku Supercomputer</a>? We now have 8 Fugaku nodes (<a href="https://www.fujitsu.com/global/products/computing/servers/supercomputer/specifications/">Fujitsu FX700</a>), available in CHI@TACC! Each of these nodes has a 48 core ARM A64FX CPU, 32 GiB of HBM2 memory, 512GB of NVMe storage, and HDR100 Infiniband. Notably, the high-bandwidth memory and non-x86 architecture are hard to find in other systems. <a href="https://www.tacc.utexas.edu/systems/frontera">TACC’s Frontera Supercomputer</a> originally procured these for evaluation, but they’re now available for general use in Chameleon.</p>

<p> </p>

<p dir="ltr">You can find them in our <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/hardware/">hardware browser</a> by selecting “site: tacc” and “Platform Type: aarch64”</p>

<p dir="ltr"><b id="docs-internal-guid-12479a9a-7fff-ca08-6192-b552c2a69b6d"><img height="172" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/Q-lb6AnSXuytR6KQfea6GzBCbYCdssY5_Byl463bm3T7-MGDoQFdfXqF1oXpJ5GbfSXUX2sakribSPou1ukd16eIYb4S-GjnNByz4Xrp2t8L2UMY9DXFkoSX6JKB5MQ_NZl79FtNLpTb28KfTyL0AgTBgyEru9DbMGaE37lD7rrG4YUZxkafcjmw" width="232"></b></p>

<p> </p>

<p dir="ltr">Or, reserve them through <a href="https://chi.tacc.chameleoncloud.org/project/leases/">Horizon at CHI@TACC</a> with the “compute_arm64” node type.</p>
