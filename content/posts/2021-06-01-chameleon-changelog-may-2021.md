<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Dear Chameleon users,</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Just a quick update from us, as we are hard at work researching which portable air conditioner to buy, err, I mean, prepping for the installation of a lot of fancy new hardware for you as part of Chameleon's next phase of operations.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Tell us about your publications on Chameleon!</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> On the Chameleon user portal you can now use the Publications dashboard to upload citation information for any publications you've utilized Chameleon resources for. When requesting renewal or recharge of the allocations, we may ask you to upload this list, so keeping it up to date now can save you time later! To help you out, we added a few things this month. You can now edit or remove publications you've previously submitted, and additionally have added support for all regular BibTex publication types (go </span><a href="https://www.bibtex.com/e/entry-types/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">here</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> if you're curious about what type applies to your publication.)</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Chameleon on the Edge: Early User period starting this month.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> We are hard at work on bringing edge and IoT capabilities to the testbed in a variety of forms and will start the Early User period this month with a couple of introductory webinars. We've created an “edgy users” </span><a href="https://groups.google.com/g/chameleon-edge-users" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">mailing list</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> that you can join to follow the latest developments and announcements, get support for these preview capabilities, and generally share knowledge with your fellow researchers in the IoT and edge fields -- we hope to see many of you there! </span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Chameleon User Survey.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> As part of Phase 3 we’re looking to improve the Chameleon experience and make the system generally better over the next 4 years. This is your chance to guide hardware purchases, influence Chameleon capabilities and change priorities in our roadmap. To help us with this effort, we would like to invite you to take this </span><a href="https://bit.ly/34E5N64" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">10-15 minute survey</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">, and share your experiences, desires, pain points and anything else to help us build a better Chameleon for you! The survey will be open till the end of June. </span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">Chameleon is participating in the </span><a href="https://sc21.supercomputing.org/submit/reproducibility-initiative" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">SuperComputing '21 Reproducibility Initiative</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> We'd like to draw your attention to the SC21 reproducibility initiative, which encourages and rewards researchers who are able to "package" their experiments as reproducible artifacts. As users of Chameleon, you can use Jupyter and the Trovi sharing portal to package your experiment setup, as well as the execution of the experiment. For an example, see this reproducible experiment for the </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/experiment/share/15" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">LinnOS storage system</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> (and learn more from </span><a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2020/11/01/chameleon-and-reproducibility-linnos-case-study/" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">this case study</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;"> previously published on our blog.) There is an upcoming webinar demonstrating these capabilities, which you can get more information about from the </span><a href="https://sc21.supercomputing.org/submit/reproducibility-initiative" style="text-decoration: none;"><span style="">SC'21 site</span></a><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; color: #000000; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></p>
