<p><big>Visit our <a href="https://reproduciblehpc.org">workshop website</a>!</big></p>

<p>We are excited to announce that registration is now open for the <strong>Community Workshop on Practical Reproducibility in HPC</strong>, scheduled for <strong>November 18, 2024</strong> in Atlanta, GA, supported by the <a href="http://repeto.cs.uchicago.edu"><strong>REPETO project</strong></a>. This workshop, which also serves as the 5th Chameleon User Meeting, aims to address the unique challenges and immense potential of reproducibility in High-Performance Computing (HPC) and systems research.</p>

<h2>Early Bird Discount Available!</h2>

<p>Take advantage of our early bird registration rate of just <strong>$20</strong> if you register before September 30, 2024. After this date, the regular registration fee will be $100.</p>

<h2>Workshop Highlights:</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Keynote speeches by distinguished speakers including Torsten Hoefler (ETH Zurich) and Kate Keahey (Argonne National Laboratory)</li>
	<li>Panel discussions with authors and reviewers from major HPC conference reproducibility initiatives</li>
	<li>Networking opportunities with leading researchers and practitioners in HPC</li>
	<li>Hands-on sessions exploring tools and methodologies for practical reproducibility</li>
</ul>

<p>This workshop is strategically scheduled alongside the <a href="http://sc24.supercomputing.org">premier annual conference showcasing the latest innovations in supercomputing technology</a>, making it an unmissable event for anyone involved in HPC research and development.</p>

<h2>Who Should Attend:</h2>

<ul>
	<li>HPC researchers and practitioners</li>
	<li>Participants in reproducibility initiatives</li>
	<li>Educators interested in HPC education</li>
	<li>Students and early-career researchers</li>
	<li>Tool and platform developers focused on reproducibility</li>
</ul>

<p>Don't miss this opportunity to be part of the conversation shaping the future of reproducibility in HPC. <a href="https://www.eventbrite.com/e/community-workshop-on-practical-reproducibility-in-hpc-tickets-1000658535617">Register now</a> to secure your spot and take advantage of the early bird discount!</p>

<p>For more information, visit our <a href="https://reproduciblehpc.org">workshop website</a> or contact us at <a href="mailto:contact@chameleoncloud.org">contact@chameleoncloud.org</a>.</p>
