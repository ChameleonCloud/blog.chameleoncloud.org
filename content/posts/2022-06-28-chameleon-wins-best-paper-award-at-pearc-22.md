<p>Chameleon is proud to accept the award for Best Paper at the upcoming <a href="https://pearc.acm.org/pearc22/">PEARC '22</a> conference! Our paper <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public/cb/a0/cba02f95-3697-450f-b3a3-f6e43666e13a/paper_3530770.pdf">Migrating towards Single Sign-On and Federated Identity</a> won the award, describing our challenging experience in migrating our identity system. </p>

<p>We also received a lovely article about our paper from the University of Chicago computer science department, which you can read <a href="https://cs.uchicago.edu/news/single-sign-on-migration-for-chameleon-project-receives-pearc-best-paper-award/">here</a>.</p>

<p>And if you happen to be at PEARC '22, please feel free to <a href="https://web.cvent.com/event/7b4eff30-d151-4cf4-91f0-b68c4a6c88c9/websitePage:645d57e4-75eb-4769-b2c0-f201a0bfc6ce">say hello to us, or ask questions</a>!</p>
