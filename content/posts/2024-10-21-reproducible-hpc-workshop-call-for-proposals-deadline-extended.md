<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words">We are extending the deadline for submitting presentation proposals for the upcoming Workshop on Practical Reproducibility in HPC/Fifth Chameleon User Meeting. The new deadline is:</p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words"><strong>Monday, October 21, 2024 at 11:59 PM (anywhere on Earth)</strong></p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words">This extension gives you an extra day to finalize and submit your proposals. <strong>Remember, the top 10 selected abstracts will receive travel reimbursement of up to $1,500 for one presenting author per abstract.</strong></p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words"><strong>Details at: <a href="http://reproduciblehpc.org">https://reproduciblehpc.org</a> </strong></p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words">Please send your submissions to <a href="mailto:presentations@chameleoncloud.org">presentations@chameleoncloud.org</a>.</p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words">We look forward to receiving your innovative proposals on HPC reproducibility!</p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words"><strong>About the workshop</strong>:</p>

<p class="whitespace-pre-wrap break-words">The upcoming Chameleon User Meeting focuses on advancing reproducibility in High-Performance Computing (HPC). This gathering brings together researchers, educators, and practitioners to share experiences, challenges, and best practices in creating reproducible HPC experiments. Participants will explore innovative approaches to packaging and reproducing complex computational experiments, discuss obstacles encountered, and propose strategies for improving reproducibility support in HPC environments. The meeting aims to generate insights that will shape future directions in HPC research and education, emphasizing the integration of reproducibility into mainstream practices. Outcomes will include a comprehensive report capturing the community's collective knowledge and recommendations for practical reproducibility in HPC.</p>
