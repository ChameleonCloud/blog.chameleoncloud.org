<p>Dear Chameleon Community,</p>

<p>We are excited to announce the launch of the 2024 Chameleon User Survey!</p>

<p><strong>Please access the survey here</strong>: <a href="https://uchicago.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_6R2VlvbmnkwFgSa">https://uchicago.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_6R2VlvbmnkwFgSa</a>. <strong>Please submit your responses before Feb. 6, 2024!</strong></p>

<p>Your participation is invaluable in shaping the future of the Chameleon Cloud platform. This survey is your opportunity to voice your needs, experiences, and suggestions, directly impacting the development of new hardware features and capacity improvements on Chameleon.</p>

<p>Your insights are crucial for us to tailor Chameleon to better suit your project requirements. By dedicating some time (approx. 10-15 minutes) to complete the survey, you're not just sharing feedback; you're actively contributing to a community-driven effort to enhance our collective capabilities.</p>

<p>Thank you for your continued support and engagement.</p>
