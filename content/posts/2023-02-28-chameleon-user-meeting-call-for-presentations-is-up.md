<p><strong>The Call for Presentations at the Chameleon User Meeting is out! </strong>We invite you to share your resaerch as well as your experiences working with the Chameleon testbed! As in prior years, to facilitate this discussion we are soliciting proposals for brief presentations (15-20 minutes) based on your resaerch or education work with Chameleon. The deadline to submit presentation proposals is <b id="docs-internal-guid-d9278dc8-7fff-c0e8-7c81-a78d026f79a4">April, 3rd, 2023 </b>and as in prior years the top ten submissions will be awarded travel support to the meeting. <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/chameleon-cloud-users-meeting/user-meeting-2023/?tk5VAFwlx9IKSoczFi3XlNv4xbGeNn8pzAlCu9555gi2238lu36PAj8jQgtch3U">Read more about how to submit a presentation proposal for the Chameleon User Meeting. </a></p>

<p>We have also opened registration for the meeting with early registration ending on April 1st. The meeting will be held in the <a href="https://cs.uchicago.edu">Crerar Library, home to the Computer Science Department</a>. As in the years before, the User Meeting will consist of user presentations and keynotes on the first day, followed by a reception in University of Chicago's new <a href="https://davidrubensteinforum.uchicago.edu">Rubenstein Forum</a> overlooking the campus. On the second day, we will hold four parallel events: Chameleon tutorials, Chameleon site operators (CHI-in-a-Box) meeting, mini-symposium on education using Chameleon, and a reproducibility hackathon. We hope to see all in the Chameleon friends and family there!</p>
