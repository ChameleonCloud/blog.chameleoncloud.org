title: The Chameleon Technology Preview is ready

<p>We are delighted to announce that the Chameleon Technology Preview is ready for users (Happy Easter!)</p>

<p>All Chameleon users can start using the Technology Preview; to start, follow the instructions in the <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/docs/user-guides/technology-preview-user-guide/">User Guide</a>.</p>

<p>The Technology Preview provides a first version of the bare metal reconfiguration capabilities of Chameleon: resource discovery, reservation, bare-metal provisioning, and monitoring. For the time being, we have deployed it on a modest number of nodes of the Alamo FutureGrid hardware.</p>

<p>Come and give it a shot — and let us know about any and all things that need improvement!</p>
