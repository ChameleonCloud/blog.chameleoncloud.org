<p>We are happy to announce that the Chameleon project has been extended for another 4 years! </p>

<p>That’s four more years of working with a creative and talented user community that always wants to go someplace impossible – and takes us with them! </p>

<p>The next four years will bring us integration with IoT, support for more innovative networking experiments, innovative new hardware, and even more support for reproducibility and experiment sharing. Read all about it in: https://www.cs.uchicago.edu/news/article/chameleon-phase-three/</p>

<p>We are SO looking forward to continuing to provide a platform for your research -- and learning about all the hot and cool things you do! </p>
