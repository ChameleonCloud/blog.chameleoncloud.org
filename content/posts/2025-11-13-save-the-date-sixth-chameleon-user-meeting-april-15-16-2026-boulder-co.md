<p><strong>Save the date and join us in Boulder, Colorado for the 6th Chameleon User Meeting from April 15-16th, 2026!</strong></p>

<p>The <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/chameleon-cloud-users-meeting/sixth-chameleon-user-meeting/">Chameleon User Meeting</a> is an in-person forum held over two days for users to discuss their research and education projects, share experiences of working with the Chameleon testbed, solve challenges together, and propose new features that will make their experiments and education projects easier. The User Meeting will have features to interest Chameleon newbies as well as veterans, end-users and operators, and researchers and educators. For our sixth meeting, we are grateful to co-host with the <a href="https://ncar.ucar.edu/">National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR)</a> at its main campus in Boulder, CO. NCAR recently moved from an associate site to join as an official Chameleon site and partner as part of <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/blog/2024/08/01/chameleon-testbed-secures-12-million-in-funding-for-phase-4/">Chameleon Phase 4</a>.</p>

<p>We will post all details about the Chameleon User Meeting, including calls for presentations, registration, venue and travel, and more on our <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/chameleon-cloud-users-meeting/sixth-chameleon-user-meeting/">6th User Meeting webpage</a>. We'll continue to announce updates on our blog as well.</p>
