<p>Much of the HPC class I teach at the University of Piemonte Orientale and University of Turin in Italy utilizes the Chameleon testbed. I utilize Chameleon for my course because it’s a valuable resource to get practice with Cloud Computing in a real environment by using a reliable platform for free.</p>

<p dir="ltr"><a href="http://dcs.di.unipmn.it/mex/?Teaching:High_Performance_Computing_Class">My website outlines resources</a> that I hope other educators will find useful for their curriculum.
